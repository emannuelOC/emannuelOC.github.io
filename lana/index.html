Lana

Some history

Lana is a project I've thinking about and working on since 2013 (it's been almost 5 years now). Since then, the ideas about the app have changed a lot. It all started when I joined the Apple Developer Academy, and we were told that, at some point in the future, we'd have to choose a "Big Idea" to work on and I decided I wanted to create an app about brain health.

2013/2014: Proto-Lana

At first, it was supposed to be a sort of helper for speech therapists to work with aphasia patients, so they could use less paper.

2015: Mobility

Then, as the project evolved, I noticed it could be more and I considered adding the functionality to allow patients to exercise at home and let the doctors track their progress.

2016: Personalization

As time went by I noticed that the tests and exercises were "one-size-fits-all" solutions and patients - specially neurological patients - had very idiossyncratic needs. So I decided Lana should allow doctors and therapists to customize the tests and exercises according to each patient's needs.

2017: Sleep

For some unclear reasons, in 2017 I didn't work on Lana as much as I could and the ideas I had about it were quietly asleep. During that year I've learned a lot both on iOS development and Machine Learning. And that allowed me to come up with a new idea.

2018: Intelligent Assistent

Now, I'm rewriting Lana from scratch, only this time it's supposed to be a very different app. It still has the advantages of mobility and personalization, but now it is somewhat proactive.

The app

In this new concept, the user will receive a notification (a message) every morning, remininding him/her about their daily exercises. Then, as he opens the app, it will look like a chat, where he will interact with Lana, answering questions, drawing things, typing, speaking, standing, walking, etc. for five minutes. The app will track the patients score and when it notices some alterations, cognitive or otherwise, it will let the appointed doctor know, so he/she can create a custom test for the patient or even call him/her up in the chat or personaly to check their health.

Monetization

Lana's main clients aren't the patients or doctors anymore. Now we will focus on health insurance companies and hospitals, whose main value out of the app will be to detect neurological diseases earlier so they can be treated early, reducing the expenses with the treatment.

My clients will be able to offer discounts for patients who are willing to take five minutes of their days to "talk to Lana" and have their brain health checked continually.
